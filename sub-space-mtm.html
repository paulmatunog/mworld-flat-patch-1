Mission to Mars

Mars, the red planet, has always held a special place in our imagination. It is one of the closest planets to the Earth, and it is eerily familiar in many ways. There are hills, valleys, plains, dust devils, morning frosts and polar ice caps on Mars.

Apart from our own world, Mars is the planet that has been explored most extensively by robots sent from Earth. And Mars will be the first planet visited by interplanetary astronauts.

It is time to take a mission to Mars.
